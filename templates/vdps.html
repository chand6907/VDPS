About VDPS

The Vehicle Detection and Prediction System (VDPS) is a groundbreaking solution designed specifically for one-way mountain roads. By leveraging advanced sensors, real-time data processing, and predictive analytics, our system addresses critical safety concerns in areas where traditional traffic management systems fall short.
